About NurQode

NurQode was founded with a vision to provide premium web development services across the Middle East and North America. Our name combines "Nur" (light in Arabic) with "Qode" (code), representing our mission to illuminate the digital landscape with exceptional coding solutions.

With teams across multiple countries in the region, we understand the unique needs and preferences of Middle Eastern and North American businesses. Our developers are skilled in creating websites that not only look stunning but also perform exceptionally in local markets.

We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, within budget, and exceeding client expectations. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for businesses from Qatar to Canada.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our web development services

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Typically, our projects take 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. A basic 5-page website takes about 2-3 weeks, while e-commerce sites or custom web applications may take 4-6 weeks.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

+

Yes! We offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. All our pricing plans include initial support, and we offer ongoing maintenance contracts.

Can you work with businesses outside the Middle East and North America?

+

While we specialize in Middle Eastern and North American markets, we welcome clients from around the world. Our team is fluent in English and Arabic, and we've successfully delivered projects for international clients.

What technologies do you use for web development?

+

We use modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and various CMS platforms. We choose the best technology stack based on your specific project requirements.

Do you offer custom web applications?

+

Absolutely! Our Enterprise package includes custom web application development. We build tailored solutions for businesses needing specific functionality beyond standard websites.
